<h2><em>Reporting Requirements</em></h2>
<p><em>• A notifiable cybersecurity incident, i.e., a cybersecurity incident in respect of any applicable computer or computer system under the control of the healthcare provider, which is prescribed, e.g. ransomware</em>
</p>
<p><em>• A notifiable data breach that (is likely to) result in significant harm to an affected individual or is (likely to be) of a significant scale</em>
</p>
<p><em>o Breach involves sensitive health information, or</em>
</p>
<p><em>o ≥500 individuals affected</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p><em>• Healthcare providers are required to:</em>
</p>
<p><em>o Provide an initial notification to MOH within 2 hours of a confirmed incident</em>
</p>
<p><em>o Provide an incident report within 14 days of initial notification</em>
</p>
<p><em>• IT vendors are required to notify the HIA entity of suspected data breach without delay</em>
</p>
