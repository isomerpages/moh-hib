<h2>Reporting Timelines</h2>
<p><strong>Healthcare providers</strong> are required to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Provide an initial notification to MOH within 2 hours of a confirmed incident</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide an incident report within 14 days of initial notification</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>IT vendors</strong> are required to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>notify the HIA entity of suspected data breach without delay</p>
</li>
</ul>
