<p>Following the 28 March 2026 Singapore Medical Association (SMA) Health
Information Act (HIA) Webinar, the Ministry of Health (MOH) and Agency
for Integrated Care (AIC) will be conducting two additional webinars with&nbsp;the
same content for Outpatient Medical Service (OMS) licensees on 14 April
and 12 May 2026, 1pm to 2:30pm. These sessions will cover the same topics
as the SMA webinar, including:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>HIA obligations and the implementation timelines;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>NEHR contribution and Cybersecurity and Data Security requirements; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Implementation support that is available</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>OMS licensees may register for either one of these upcoming webinars
<a href="https://ddei5-0-ctp.trendmicro.com/wis/clicktime/v1/query?url=https%3a%2f%2fsynapxe.zoom.us%2fwebinar%2fregister%2fWN%5fUpgde7%2dLTheXsDaCF19sjw%23%2fregistration&amp;umid=320ABA2D-4E03-4906-9AE0-393E81F86F96&amp;auth=ce6a3f6d14c1e3e3f878a8a607dd7ee2cb794b08-ab742c67885e02c7012de2cb40ab3035d1427abd" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>here</u>
</a>.</p>
<p>Please select your preferred date and time in the registration page. Upon
successful registration, you will receive a confirmation email with a unique
webinar link, as well as a reminder email before the webinar. Please use
your dedicated link to login.</p>
