<p>Explain the three Objectives (in public-friendly terms):</p>
<p>1. Objective 1 provides the legislative basis for (i) mandatory contribution
of selected health information to the NEHR, and (ii) access to the NEHR
for patient care and other specified purposes.</p>
<p>2. Objective 2 enables sharing of identifiable non-NEHR health information
between prescribed entities (e.g., clusters, public agencies) for specified
use cases.</p>
<p>3. Objective 3 sets out the Cybersecurity and Data Security Requirements
imposed on HIA entities1 and providers of Health Information Management
Systems (HIMS)</p>
