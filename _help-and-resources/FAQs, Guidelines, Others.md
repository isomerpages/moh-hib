<h2>Key Elements of the HIA</h2>
<p>The HIA introduces important changes to how your health information is
managed and shared across Singapore's healthcare system. This infographic
linked below explains how these changes will improve your healthcare experience
through better coordination between healthcare providers and enhanced support
programmes.</p>
<p>Understanding these changes will help you make informed decisions about
your healthcare and benefit from the improved services available to you.</p>
